About

Lingo Apps are high performance Android and Iphone text input tools for foreign languages, such as Thai, Mandarin Chinese, Cantonese, and Japanese, using the English keyboard and Romanized vocabulary equivalents of the respective language. Accuracy in spelling and vocabulary can be assured by continually verifying your selection along with Lingo's built-in dictionary. Built for fluent speakers of the language and beginners alike, through repetition and familiarity, text input can be done faster by using the Lingo App than text entered through the language's native keyboard.



Romanization

About Romanization
For any language you wish to study or learn, you usually take a style of writing you are familiar with and use it to help pronounce the new language. In the case of the Roman (Latin) script alphabet (used in a variety languages, such as English, Spanish, French, or German), this process is called Romanization. Romanization serves to help the language speaker in pronunciation of unfamiliar vocabulary and also as a memorization aid, because it helps the speaker notate and visualize the pronunciation so it can be recalled for later use.

Non-phonetic Languages
Complicating things a bit further, not all written languages are phonetic languages (referencing Chinese-based languages and parts of both Thai and Japanese languages, in particular), meaning unfamiliar vocabulary of the language exist in a written form that can not be sounded out by their appearance alone. In this function, Romanization also acts in a crucial supporting role to help clarify ambiguous pronunciations.

Stigma
Because of the reliance on Romanization in early stages of language study, it has a stigma of being an elementary learning tool, subsequently discarded soon after you are able to properly learn the writing system. Likewise, it also has a stigma of being a tool of the lazy and cast aside as a just a crutch for those who don't have the time, interest, or resources to properly learn the language's writing system.

Our perspective
Here at LLT we like to consider Romanization as a foundational building block of language study. Romanization should remain in the toolbox of any multi-linguist, both as a learning aid and because of its direct correlation with pronunciation, both in familiarity and memorization.

Data Privacy

LLT is obsessed with privacy, security, keeping your devices safe from harm and keeping your experience AD-free. We make every attempt to make sure that LLT user data is not a commodity to be bought or sold. All LLT Apps are self contained and run in the native code of your device to ensure the best performance possible. All of our apps are completely functional without internet data transfer, so they will always be functional whether or not you have mobile data or a wifi connection. Use our apps whenever and however you like with full privacy. Our customers should demand nothing less.
